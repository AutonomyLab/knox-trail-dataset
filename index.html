Hetereogeneous Robots in the Wild:

The Knox Trail Dataset


The Knox Trail Dataset consists of approximately 50 GB of sensor data recorded on three heterogeneous robots with different sensor packages at Knox Mountain Park, British Columbia, Canada. All three robots traversed the 2km trail at the same time, and sometimes appear in the other robots' sensors. Sensors include cameras, single- and multi-beam scanning laser rangefinders, GPS, inertial measurements and wheel encoders. Sensor calibration is deliberately approximate, reflecting long-term situations where robots must be robust to imperfect and changing sensor parameters. We provide the data in the form of ROS bag files, JPEG image files, and CSV text files.
